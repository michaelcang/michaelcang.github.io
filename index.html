Welcome to my page about pet dogs. My name is Michael. I love dogs even though I don't have one. I made this page for people who are interested to start adopting a dog or just want to know more about pet dogs.

About Dogs

Dogs are the most popular pet in the world. They are loyal, cute and they can protect you from danger. There are many kinds of pet dogs. Adopting a dog is a very big commitment, so make sure you are ready before adopting.

Top 5 Most Popular Pet Dogs

  1. Shiba Inu

    2. A very cute and friendly with human pet dog from the islands of Japan.

  2. Labrador Retriever

    3. This dog is very popular because of it's calm demeanor. However, if properly trained this pet dog can fiercely protect it's owner.

  3. Pembroke Welsh Corgi

    4. This pet dog is one of the most friendliest breed. Moreover it's small build make the dog easy to carry around by the owner.

  4. German Sheperd

    5. This is a relatively new breed of pet dog. This breed is very loyal to it's owner and also can learn many tricks.

  5. Beagle

    6. This breed is one of the smartest breed, so this dog is often used for security dog by airport authorities.
